# Darpan-Teleprompter-Account-Deletion-Instructions
To delete your Darpan Teleprompter account and associated data
To delete your Darpan Teleprompter account and associated data, please follow these steps
i.Revoke App Access from your Google Account: Your Darpan Teleprompter account is managed through your Google Account. To delete your account, you must revoke the app's access permissions from your Google security settings.
ii.Visit the "Third-party apps with account access" section of your Google Account here: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
iii.Find "Darpan Teleprompter" in the list of apps.
iv.Click on "Darpan Teleprompter" and then click the "Remove Access" button.What happens next? Revoking access will prevent the app from accessing your Google Account information. The unique user ID associated with your account will be deleted from our systems. Any locally stored scripts on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the app.If you have any questions, please contact our support at [Your Support Email].
